Privacy Policy for Wobble

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Wobble ("we", "our", "us") is an English language practice app that connects users for live audio conversations. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Google account information - When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address.
  • Language level - The English proficiency level you select in the app.
  • Audio during calls - Audio from your microphone is transmitted to your conversation partner during a live call session.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and create your account.
  • To match you with a suitable conversation partner based on your language level.
  • To enable live audio calls between matched users.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required to operate the service (Google Sign-In, LiveKit for audio infrastructure).

Microphone Access

The app requests microphone permission solely to enable audio calls with your matched partner. Microphone access is only active during an active call session.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your Wobble account and all associated data at any time by emailing workpav17@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account". We will delete your account, including your email address, language level, and call history, within 30 days of your request.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as you use the app. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: workpav17@gmail.com
